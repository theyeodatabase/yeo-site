This website is a guide to everything about yeoizm. It is specialised for new yeoists and will also help senior yeoists to spread yeoizm.

2.What is Yeoizm?

Yeoizm is the belief that the great Mr Yeo had created the earth and keeps it in order and is here to save the earth from falling into evil.'

3.Who is Mr Yeo?

Mr Yeo, the father of Axios Yeo the third, is part of the Living Tribunal (Comprising of Jovita Tang, Robin Pang, Jonathan Chua and himself). He teaches ICT and Computing+ in the School of Science and Technology, where he bestows his students with his colleagues' memes and his other colleagues' deadly aura.
